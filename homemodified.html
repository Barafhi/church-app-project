A NO BVA KHA MUNWALI

Idzi nyimbo dzi bva kha nyimbo dzo ḓoweleaho dza Tshisuthu na dzinwe dzi nobva kha nyimbo dza “Phalaphala”.
Ri livhuwa vhafunzi na vhathu vhanzhi Vhe Vha khuṱhedzela uri mushumo uyu u bveledzwe. Ri elelwa maipfi a ṱhuṱhuwedzo a no bva kha munna wa vhane muingameli vhafunzi vho Emmanuel Muthivhi Makhuvha Vhe Vha thoma mushumo uyu,
Bugu iyi ndi nwala tshi tshihumbudzo tsha khonani yanga Vho-M.M. Sinoamadi Vhe Vha lovha nga 27/04/2000. Ya dovha ya Vha tshihumbudzo tsha nwana wanga Maemu Maiwashe we a ri siya nga 12/12/2004. Ri ḓo vha humbula misi yoṱhe.
Kereke ya AME i ḓivhea sa kereke ya u imba, Nyimbo dzi na tshiga tshihulwane kha mushumo wa kereke ino.
Ndi khumbulo yashu uri Luimbo ulu lu ḓo funga ḓenzhe mbiluni dza vhane vha ḓo lu shumisa

Maṱamela Joseph Maiwashe
